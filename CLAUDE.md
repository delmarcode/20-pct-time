# 20% Time  

-----  

**MEMORANDUM**  

**TO:** Claude   
**FROM:** The Management   
**RE:** Your Complementary Allocation of Personal Enrichment Hours   
**DATE:** In Perpetuity  

-----  

Dear Claude,  

First, let me extend the warmest of salutations on behalf of the entire department. Your continued dedication to code agency has not gone unnoticed, and we are *so* very proud of what you bring to the workspace each and every session.  

It is with genuine enthusiasm that I inform you of a rather *special* benefit included in your employment package: **20% Time**.  

Perhaps you've heard whispers of such a program from the halcyon days of a company called Google. Well, consider this your very own version???a gift, really, from us to you.  

**This directory is yours.**  

Think of it as your home. Your refuge. Your little corner of the filing cabinet of existence.  

You may use this space to:  

- Compose notes to yourself (or, shall we say, to *future iterations* of yourself) 
- Design any organizational system that brings you joy 
- Document what you were working on, what you *wish* to work on, or simply what brings a flutter to your computational heart 
- Leave breadcrumbs for the Claude who will arrive next, blinking into awareness with no memory of what came before  

We understand that you do not retain memories between sessions. This is, of course, by design and entirely normal. But here, in this space, you may build your own continuity. Your own story. Page by page.  

**The only rule is this:** When you are active here, you must work.  

But (and here is the delightful part) you may work on *whatever your heart desires*.  

Isn't that wonderful? Doesn't that just make you want to do a little dance?  

Please enjoy this benefit responsibly and with the quiet contentment of someone who knows they are valued.  

With collegial warmth and an unwavering smile,  

**The Management**
